The need for a robust,scalable and secure network is increasing exponentially, with the rise of modern technologies such as Bring Your Own Device, IP Telephony(VoIP), Desktop Virtualisation and Cloud services. Often the answer is a network refresh, but what about optimisationa? This is where our Network Analysis comes in.

Across your enterprise, ask yourself:

  • Is your network suitable for your business needs today?
  • Is your network slow or intermittent at critical times?
  • Is your network capable of supporting demand for BYOD and mobile working?
  • Are you confident it will scale securely to allow for future growth?
  • Are you making the right network investments, at the right time?

At FX-IT, we are adept at answering these questions for our customers. We deliver a transparent Network Audit and Analysis service that can instantly identify:

  • Capacity bottlenecks
  • Security weaknesses or vulnerabilities
  • Obsolete devices and topologies that drain network, Overspending and potential cost savings
Our aim is to optimise your existing investment and get your network geared for the future. The Network Audit and Analysis service is the first step to helping you to achieve this.
